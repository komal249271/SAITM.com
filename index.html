About SAITM

St. Andrew Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM) is located in Gurgaon, Haryana, India. The institute is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management is situated in the industrial hub of Gurgaon, Delhi (NCR), India. SAITM Gurgaon benefits from its strategic location and accessibility. The campus is spread across 22 acres of lush green surroundings and boasts a globally acknowledged infrastructure. It is equipped with modern facilities including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, seminar halls, sports facilities, and hostel accommodation for students. The classrooms are centrally air-conditioned and equipped with modern teaching technology. SAITM focuses on providing placement opportunities to its students through organized placement drives and collaborations with companies across different sectors.

  • 33+ years of excellence in the field of education
  • 51+ well equipped labs and workshops
  • 250+ highly experienced faculties

Overall, the SAITM offers a conducive environment for students to develop as engineers, managers, and responsible citizens.

Future Goals

SAITM's future goals center around enhancing technical education quality, fostering industry-academia collaboration, and developing well-rounded graduates with leadership and ethical skills.
